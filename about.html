About

I am a Ph.D. student in Natural Language Processing at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Roi Reichart. My research studies the behavior of large language models and how AI systems act and make decisions in complex settings. I am particularly interested in human-like properties of AI systems and in approaches that draw on insights from psychology, education, and cognition to better understand and evaluate these models.
